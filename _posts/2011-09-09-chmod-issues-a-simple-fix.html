If (for whatever reason) you mess up the CHMOD permissions for the files and folders,Â you can run these commands to recursively "reset" all files and folders to their defaults.

find /home/david/Documents/ -type d -print0 | xargs -0 chmod 755
find /home/david/Documents/ -type f -print0 | xargs -0 chmod 644
