A bit of ancient history for me, the Corrodere E-Learning platform was my first truly successful project. I designed and developed this back in 2007 for my fathers company, Digitalized.

He continues to manage it, with my original code largely unchanged. It is still a highly profitable platform for all involved.

Corrodere is the leading E-Learning platform for the coating-specialist industry. That is, industrial painting and maintenance.

I architected the platform to have a number of features:

  • Can run offline - useful for offshore environments without broadband
  • A translation module - the content is available in 8 languages
  • A comprehensive user management system
  • PDF certificates are generated and issued on the site
  • Hundreds of animations explain technical details and hazardous situations
  • Thousands of short videos demonstrate techniques and procedures

The platform is fully white-labelled - this allows companies to hand-pick modules and units to suit their individual needs. They can then theme their site to fit their branding.
